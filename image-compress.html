How Our Image Compressor Works

  2. Adjust compression settings using the sliders
  3. Click "Compress Images" to process your file

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats. You can compress these formats or convert between them.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the current limit is 10MB per image. This ensures fast processing in your browser.

How does the compression work?

For JPEG images, we adjust the quality level. For PNG, we use optimization techniques. WEBP compression uses both lossy and lossless methods.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server.

Is this service really free?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges.
